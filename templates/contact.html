About Our Canteen

Who We Are

The University Canteen Management System is designed to provide students and staff with a fast, simple, and convenient food ordering experience. Our canteen offers a variety of healthy, delicious, and affordable meals every day. Using this system, customers can browse the menu, place orders, and receive bills quickly. Administrators can efficiently manage food items, orders, sales, and reports.
